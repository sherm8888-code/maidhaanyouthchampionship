Tournament Rules

Quick summary of the key regulations and prizes for the 2026 championship.

Squad Size

  • ✓ Players: 12 per team
  • ✓ Officials: 4 per team
  • ✓ Locals: Max 1 out-of-island player

Match Format

  • ✓ Time: Two 20-min halves
  • ✓ Subs: Unlimited rolling subs
  • ✓ Breaks: One 1-min timeout/half

Eligibility & Fees

  • ✓ U16: Born after Jan 1, 2010
  • ✓ U13: Born after Jan 1, 2013
  • ✓ Fee: 100% Free Entry
